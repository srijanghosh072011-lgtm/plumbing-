FAQ

Calgary's Most Common Plumbing Questions.

Straight answers from our master plumbers and gas fitters.

Are you really available 24 hours a day?
Yes — a real human answers our phone 24/7/365. Holidays, weekends, 3 a.m. on a Tuesday — call us. We dispatch the closest available technician immediately and aim to be on-site within 60 minutes anywhere in Calgary.
Do you charge a fee just to come out and quote?
No. On-site quotes for regular service calls are 100% free. For after-hours emergency calls, there's a small dispatch fee that's waived if you proceed with the repair. Everything is disclosed before we book.
What areas around Calgary do you service?
All of Calgary plus Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, Okotoks, Strathmore, Langdon, Bragg Creek, Springbank, De Winton, Heritage Pointe, and Black Diamond. Just call — if we don't service your area, we'll refer you to someone trustworthy who does.
Do you give upfront pricing or charge by the hour?
We use upfront flat-rate pricing for every job, big or small. You'll see the exact price before any work begins and it won't change. No hourly meter ticking while we work — no incentive to drag the job out.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Every tech is licensed in Alberta, background-checked, and fully insured. We carry $5M liability coverage and WCB. We're also bonded — your home is protected from the moment we walk in.
Do you offer financing for big jobs like furnace replacements?
Yes. We partner with Financeit to offer 0% interest plans on qualifying installs (furnaces, water heaters, large repairs). Approval takes minutes and you can do it from our truck before we start.
Do you offer warranties on your work?
Every repair we make is backed by a 2-year workmanship warranty. Installs come with full manufacturer warranties (typically 10 years on furnaces, 6+ on tanks) plus our labour guarantee. If anything goes wrong, we come back — no charge.
